About Us

Located in Seoul, we are a new artisan workshop prepared to offer the highest quality furniture. We combine tradition and innovation to deliver a distinguished finish that reflects our passion for craftsmanship. Make your house a home with Vittrigas.

Craftsmanship & Detail

Our skilled artisans focus on every detail, using time-honored techniques to ensure each piece is unique and timeless. With years of experience, we take pride in our craftsmanship, ensuring that every piece of furniture is made with care and precision.

Innovation Meets Tradition

We blend modern design with traditional materials, crafting furniture that fits contemporary homes while honoring heritage. While we are a new workshop, we have a long history of craftsmanship and dedication to quality.

From cozy chairs to elegant tables, Vittrigas helps transform living spaces into expressions of personal style and warmth. The traditional design of our furniture is meant to last for generations, making it a perfect addition to your home.

No Planet B

As humans, we owe everything to nature. That's why we need to protect it at all costs. In Vittrigas, we believe in the power of joint action. Every little gesture counts. There's no Planet B.

We are committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly materials and practices to minimize our environmental impact. Our furniture is designed not only to be beautiful but also to be kind to the planet.
